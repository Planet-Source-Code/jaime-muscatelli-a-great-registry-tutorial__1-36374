*** Famous Locations in the registry ***
The part where data is ran every time your computer starts: "HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SOFTWARE\Microsoft\Windows\CurrentVersion\Run" The AIM Screen Name Storage: "HKEY_CURRENT_USER\Software\America Online\AOL Instant Messenger (TM)\CurrentVersion\Users" The place where VB stores the info if you use the "SaveSetting" and "DeleteSetting" functions in vb: "HKEY_CURRENT_USER\Software\VB and VBA Program Settings"
